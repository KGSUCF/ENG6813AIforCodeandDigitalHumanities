Methodological Note — Human + AI Workflow
The metadata in this visualization was generated computationally: dates were parsed from source filenames, theme tags were assigned by keyword matching across the full texts, reading levels were calculated using the Flesch-Kincaid formula, and audience classifications were inferred from title and occasion keywords. The AI tool surfaced patterns across all 125 speeches simultaneously — a task that would take weeks by hand. However, several speeches resist their algorithmic categories: Lincoln's Second Inaugural is tagged “religion-morality” but functions primarily as a meditation on national guilt and reconciliation — a distinction no keyword count captures. The charts here are a map, not the territory.
